sparkleToColor

private val sparkleToColor: (AnimationData) -> Unit

Runs a Sparkle To Color animation.

Very similar to the Sparkle animation, but the LEDs are not reverted to their original color after the sparkle. At the beginning, shuffleArray is shuffled, then the LEDs are sparkled in the order given in shuffleArray.
