sparkle

private val sparkle: (AnimationData) -> Unit

Runs a Sparkle animation.

Each LED is changed to color1 for delay milliseconds before reverting to its original color. At the beginning, shuffleArray is shuffled, then the LEDs are sparkled in the order given in shuffleArray.
