sparkleToColor

@NonRepetitive private val sparkleToColor: (AnimationData) -> Unit

Runs a Sparkle To Color animation.

Very similar to the Sparkle animation, but the LEDs are not reverted to their original color after the sparkle. A separate thread is created for each pixel. Each thread waits for 0-5 seconds before sparkling its pixel.
